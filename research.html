Dissertation

My dissertation studies how quantification and plurality can be encoded in graph-based semantic representations [github repo]. The outcome includes
  • a graph language based on AMR and HLDS,
  • a model-theoretical semantics of this language,
  • a semantic parser that composes semantic graphs in CCG syntax.
