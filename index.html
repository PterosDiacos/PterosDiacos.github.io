I'm an Applied Researcher at eBay, where I build NLP models to understand the content and quality of listings.

Previously I was a PhD student at the Rutgers Department of Linguistics, where my research focussed on semantics ("what does this mean in terms of logic?"), syntax-semantics interface ("how does that meaning derive from syntax?"), and computational linguistics:

  • understanding the nature of semantic computation
  • practical natural language processing/understanding
I'm excited to wed formal theories with learning models.
